Plantae

Plantae is the kingdom of multicellular, photosynthetic organisms that produce oxygen and serve as a primary source of energy for other living organisms.

Plants are multicellular eukaryotes with cell walls made of cellulose. They use chlorophyll to capture sunlight and perform photosynthesis, producing glucose and oxygen. This kingdom includes mosses, ferns, conifers, and flowering plants. Plants provide food, oxygen, and habitats for countless organisms on Earth.

Studying plants helps scientists understand ecosystems, plant biology, and environmental conservation. Plants also play a key role in agriculture, medicine, and climate regulation.
